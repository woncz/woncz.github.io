#### 2019-09-19 等比数列求和公式
```
Sn = 1, q, q^2, q^3 ... q^n
q * Sn = q, q^2, q^3 ... q^n, q^n+1
Sn - q * Sn = 1 - q^n+1
Sn = (1 - q^n+1) / (1 - q) 其中 q != 1
```
